What We Do

We do everything in our power to help those who are suffering from mental health issues. We do this by running support groups, organizing community events, and fundraising. We are perhaps most well known for our annual walk, which once had over 200,000 participants worldwide.

Who We Are

Darkness Into Light is more than just a walk, it’s a global movement against suicide and self-harm. As our flagship fundraiser, it keeps our doors open and allows us to help more people every year, always free of charge.
